What is Miqat

Miqat is a smart calendar application that helps you manage your schedule in a completely
new way. Beyond just dates and times, Miqat brings interactive flows and a visual whiteboard
to your calendar, so you can plan, connect, and create with clarity.

Smart Intelligence

AI-powered scheduling that learns your patterns and optimizes your time automatically.

Connected Workflow

See how your tasks and events connect in a visual flow that makes sense.

Visual Planning

Transform your calendar into a creative workspace with integrated whiteboard tools.
